I am now a master student in the [School of Land Engineering](https://sle.chd.edu.cn/) of [Chang'an University (CHU)](https://www.chd.edu.cn/) (长安大学土地工程学院). I graduated from School of Land Engineering, CHU  with a bachelor's degree in land resource management (土地资源管理). Now I am pursuing my master's degree also in the  School of Land Engineering, CHU, advised by Professor [Ling Han (韩玲)](https://js.chd.edu.cn/sle/hl103/list.htm). I also collaborate with Vice Professor [Ming Liu (刘明)](https://js.chd.edu.cn/sle/lm2/list.htm).

My research interest includes land use, carbon emission and machine learning application. I have published 3 papers in journals and conferences.
